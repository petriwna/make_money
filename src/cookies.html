Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Futuristic Guide ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website at https://example.com. It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to make websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

Why do we use cookies?

We use first-party and third-party cookies for several reasons. Some cookies are required for technical reasons in order for our site to operate. Others help us understand how the site is being used, improve your experience, and personalize content.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for basic website functions and security.
  • Performance cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functional cookies: Remember choices you’ve made such as language preferences.

How can you control cookies?

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or accept cookies, delete existing cookies, or notify you when a new cookie is set.

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking, you can use the browser add-on: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Legal compliance

We comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) in Canada, and General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable. We do not use cookies to collect personally identifiable information without your consent.
